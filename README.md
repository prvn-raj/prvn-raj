## How I think about technology

- Architecture should reduce uncertainty, not create ceremony.
- A platform is valuable only when teams can build safely on top of it.
- Product decisions and technical decisions are rarely separate.
- AI is most useful when it improves judgment, speed, or accessibility.
- The best experiments answer a real question, even when the product is playful.
